Intro

With 9 years experience in ESL tutoring under my belt, I decided to make the transition to instructional design. After I finished my course from the University of California Irvine (DCE), I started working as a certified eLearning instructional designer and developer in 2021.

I'm passionate about learning and believe that we don't learn in a vacuum. When we can leverage the expertise of our colleagues and at the same time feel free to be ourselves, we learn better. That's why I prioritize the fostering of a supportive learning environment where employees feel psychologically safe and supported as they learn and grow.

I'm experienced in designing and building on-demand eLearning programs as well as hybrid training (asynchronous eLearning courses + live sessions/VILT). I have worked across diverse industries with partners like Alight Solutions, IMG Academy+ and Pinterest.

I'm pleased to meet you and can't wait for you to see some of my work. Or maybe you'd like to get to know more about me first?
